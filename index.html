Who we are

We are a group of individuals with professional backgrounds in culture, IT, architecture, research and event planing, that are dedicated to creating spaces for people to meet, interact and experience true togetherness regardless of distance, borders, mobility – or whatever else could be in the way.

Technology is awesome – but so is the real world

We use technology to expand our possibilities and to enrich our experiences. Working closely with our customers we develop bespoke solutions to create engaging concepts for conferences and education as well as cultural, corporate and social events. Efficiently connecting the physical and the virtual world, we enable lasting experience and content that remains relevant and personal.

Live Content

Sometimes you just want to enjoy your favourite concert – either live or when it suits you the best. We offer tailor made solutions for event producers to handle virtual environments, access management and interaction between people for both streaming live and recorded content.
